About Me

My journey began at St. Joseph's College Devagiri, where my M.Sc. in Computer Science opened doors to the fascinating world of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Through my internship at ICT Academy of Kerala, I discovered the power of predictive modeling, successfully developing four high-accuracy models that converted raw data into actionable insights.

The thrill of seeing algorithms come to life inspired me to push my boundaries through projects that make a difference. I created Nannavado, which reads human emotions through questions and facial recognition, built a loan eligibility system that helps financial institutions make informed decisions with 82% accuracy, and developed a tomato disease classifier that empowers farmers with early detection capabilities at 87% accuracy.

Earning recognition for my paper on IoT applications in disaster tracking has only fueled my passion for innovation at the intersection of technology and human needs. As I continue growing in this ever-evolving field, I bring not just technical knowledge but a genuine curiosity and determination to harness AI/ML for creating impactful solutions.
